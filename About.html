About Zy-tech Electronics limited

Zy-Electronics and Solar Limited is a leading supplier of electrical and lighting solutions for residential, commercial and industrial applications throughout the Kenya. Founded in 2023, we actively work to promote Local standards and are uniquely placed to cater to infrastructure projects across Kenya. With a presence in over 5 counties, we are constantly tracking market trends, and are focused on product development to meet our customers’ needs.

What we Do

We offer energy savings without compromising performance, efficiency and comfort. Our high performance lighting solutions cater to a wide range of applications such as sports, roads, office, education and industry. We are committed to providing you a comprehensive range of reliable, high quality and competitively priced products to our customers. At ZY-TECH we believe in refining our customers experience and improving performance in lots of little ways that make big differences. Our aim is to exceed the requirements of customers all over the country and to become the trusted, reliable, professional long-term partner for cost-effective and quality electrical products and lighting solutions. We aspire to achieve solutions where aesthetics, optical performance, and energy consumption are all in perfect balance and harmony.

Vision

Our vision at ZY-Tech Electronics is to leverage our expertise to become a trusted partner and recognized industry leader for electronic Products and services.

Mission

Our mission at Zy-Tech Electronics is to be a strategic electrical Product provider Partner for our clients.

Values

Our values guide our approach to all aspects of our business and everything we do for our clients, our people, and our stakeholders.
